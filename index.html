What Is Loss Control

Fewer Claims. Lower Premiums. A Safer Operation.

Loss control is a proactive risk management service where trained professionals evaluate your business for safety gaps, compliance issues, and operational vulnerabilities before they become claims.

For logging and trucking operations, documented loss control programs directly influence how carriers price your coverage. A well-managed operation with recorded safety practices earns better rates, better terms, and better claim outcomes.

Most agencies deliver a policy and stop there. Swamp Fox deploys loss control professionals into the field — your yard, your trucks, your worksite — to identify the exposures a desk review will never catch.

Our Services

What Our Loss Control Team Does

Six core services deployed to reduce your exposure, improve your safety record, and demonstrate to your carrier that your operation is well-managed.

01

Worksite Safety Audits

On-site evaluation of logging operations, equipment yards, and work zones. We identify OSHA compliance issues, equipment hazards, and procedural gaps before a claim exposes them.

02

Driver Qualification Reviews

Full review of DQ files for commercial drivers — MVR checks, medical certificates, hours-of-service logs, and drug/alcohol testing documentation. FMCSA compliance from the ground up.

03

Fleet & Equipment Inspections

Physical inspection of trucks, trailers, skidders, feller bunchers, and other equipment. Condition reports submitted to carriers as evidence of a well-maintained operation.

04

Safety Program Development

We help build or improve written safety programs tailored to your operation. Documented programs are a carrier requirement for preferred pricing and critical evidence in the event of a serious loss.

05

Claims Trend Analysis

Review of your loss history to identify frequency patterns, root causes, and corrective actions. Understanding why claims are occurring is the first step to stopping them.

06

DOT & Regulatory Compliance

Review of DOT operating authority, carrier registration, IFTA, and state-specific compliance requirements. We keep your operation legal and your premiums from being compromised by compliance gaps.

Who We Are

More Than an Insurance Agency

Swamp Fox Agency was founded in Moncks Corner, South Carolina with a mission that has never changed: provide honest, expert insurance counsel to the families, farmers, loggers, and business owners who built this region.

We have spent nearly four decades doing exactly that — and the way we do it has not changed much either. We sit across the table from you. We learn your operation. We tell you what coverage gaps you actually have, not just what will generate the highest commission.

When a claim happens, we answer the phone and we fight for you. That is not a tagline — it is the daily practice of every agent at Swamp Fox.

As the only endorsed agency of the South Carolina Timber Producers Association, we carry a responsibility to the forestry community that we take seriously in every policy we write, every consultation we deliver, and every claim we manage.

What Guides Us

The Principles We Work By

Honest Counsel

We tell clients what they need to hear, not what they want to hear. If a coverage decision carries meaningful risk, we explain it plainly. Our recommendations are based on your interests — not on commission structure or carrier relationships.

Industry Expertise

Forestry and trucking insurance is a specialty. Generic coverage from a generalist agency creates dangerous gaps. Our team studies the industries we serve so that the coverage we write actually protects the operation it is designed for.

Claims Advocacy

Our job does not end at policy issuance. When a claim occurs, we become your advocate — working with carriers to ensure fair, prompt resolution and that every applicable coverage is applied correctly and completely.
